Hybrid envelope

3-panel bladder · 2-panel shell · parallel equator seam
TPU bladder shell (2 disc) shell parallel seam layer tie support ring
  • Bladder: flat top + bottom circles + rectangular sidewall band; two circumferential welds to the band.
  • Shell: two discs, radius ~11% larger than bladder; domed inside r = R, flat annulus to equator; parallel plate seam at rim only.
  • Ring: shock-mount patches and wires on the shell equator (Rshell), not on the bladder.
  • Retention: contact adhesive on touching faces; paired webbing loops + UV zip ties on the equator annulus.
