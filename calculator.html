Assumptions: Air density 1.204 kg/m³ (20°C, 1 atm). Balloon-grade helium (97% purity, effective density 0.198 kg/m³). Net lift ≈ 1.007 kg/m³. §2.1 geometry (cylinder + bulging caps, bulge factor 1.05). Dual-layer mode: shell 2.5% linearly oversized vs bladder; uncoated face 45/80 g/m² (40D/70D), 190D+ sidewall 150 g/m². Single-layer mode: bladder thickness ignored; one TPU-coated ripstop layer holds gas & loads. TPU-coated ripstop: ~85 g/m² (40D), ~125 g/m² (70D); 210 g/m² sidewall. 15% seam/waste allowance on all material areas. Boston valve 44 g. Support ring: 8 mm solid fiberglass rod at 101 g/m. Tether: braided nylon #18 mason line at 1.0 g/m (25 kg break strength).
